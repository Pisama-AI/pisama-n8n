## The central finding (why zero cases reached the full bar)

Under webhook re-hosting, the corpus splits almost perfectly into workflows
that ALWAYS succeed on constructible input (48 — no failure to guard) and
workflows that NEVER succeed on constructible input (the 17 guarded ones —
their code reads paths a webhook wrapper cannot satisfy). The intersection a
FULL verification needs — fails on one input, succeeds on another — is empty
in this corpus. That is a property of credential-free tutorial workflows
authored for empty manual input, not of the guard machinery: the n=1 dogfood
workflow (purpose-built with a real input contract) passes the full bar on
the live instance. Consequence: the live subset ships STANDING GUARDS
(durable controls with delivered-route probes), and the corpus adds no new
'prevented' conclusions. We say this rather than lowering the bar.

## Disclosures (read before quoting any number)

- **Traffic provenance**: every execution is a real n8n engine run through a
  production webhook, but the INPUTS are controlled payloads generated by the
  campaign driver (varied values, never varied schema on the valid side). This is
  benchmark texture over real community-authored workflow logic — it is NOT
  customer traffic and must never be described as such.
- **Trigger re-attachment**: the mining harness had neutered every corpus workflow
  to a manual trigger; the campaign re-attached a webhook trigger (same node name,
  business logic byte-identical — see scripts/corpus_campaign_prepare.py). All
  other nodes, connections, and whitelisted settings are unmodified.
- **The 17 "wild" workflows** from the recall validation have no committed
  artifacts (third-party PII) and are excluded. The claim ceiling is the committed
  corpus of 70.
- **error_route non-recurrence is partly structural**: once settings.errorWorkflow
  is configured and resolvable, the error_workflow detector clears by
  construction. The evidential weight for that lane sits on the delivered-route
  probe and (where achievable) the 30-success accumulation, not on non-recurrence.
- **No outcome was concluded by a script.** Cases stop at ready-for-review; only a
  human concludes `prevented`, per case, after reviewing the evidence.
- **Rejected-probe side effects**: malformed probes fired at installed guards
  produce real failed executions that legitimately fire `n8n_node_error` /
  `n8n_missing_error_workflow` detections (disclosed dashboard noise; proven not
  to constitute recurrence for the guarded fingerprint).
- **Tier-A/live selection is biased by construction**: live-subset workflows are
  picked from those that fully verified locally. The funnel below is the
  unbiased view; the live subset is a showcase of the verified end of it.
